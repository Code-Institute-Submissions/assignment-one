The Beatles, formed in 1960, domintated the music scene for over a decade and managing to do what many musicians had failed to do prior, and conquer the American charts.


50 years on and The Beatles' musical influence is still evident in contemporary writing, production and performance styles. Their concerts remain sold-out and the band has an ever growing fanbase which is indicative of their music's ability to transcend generations.


As well as their world-wide concerts, The Beatles enjoy performing live at intimate events where they can play bespoke playlists and spend time with their fans in a more relaxed and personal environment.
